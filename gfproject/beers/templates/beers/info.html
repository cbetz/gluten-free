Gluten Free vs. Gluten Removed

Gluten Free

Beers labeled gluten-free are brewed with 100% gluten-free ingredients. Instead of malted barley, these beers use sorghum, gluten-free oats, brown rice, chestnuts, or buckwheat. Considered safe for individuals with Celiac Disease.

Gluten Removed

Beers labeled "processed or treated or crafted to remove gluten" are brewed with traditional ingredients, including malted barley. These beers have less than 20ppm (parts per million) gluten. These beers may not be safe for people with Celiac Disease. For more information see TTB Ruling Number 2012-2.
